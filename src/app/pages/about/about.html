Our Story

Coaching ka Introduction

Vijay Tuition Classes was born from a simple belief — that every student deserves a teacher who truly cares. Starting with just a handful of students from the neighbourhood, we have grown into one of Nagpur's most trusted academic coaching institutes.

We cover CBSE, SSC, ICSE & State boards across Classes 5–12. Our approach combines concept clarity, regular practice, and personalised attention. Our teaching methodology doesn't just target marks — it builds confidence, discipline, and a genuine love for learning.

Over 1200 students later, the core values remain unchanged: small batches, genuine care, transparent communication with parents, and an unwavering focus on results.

Our Journey

A Legacy of 15+ Years

From a small neighbourhood coaching class to Nagpur's most trusted academic institute.

2009 — Founded

Vijay Sir started the coaching institute from his home with just 12 students, driven by a passion for teaching and making quality education accessible to all.

2012 — Expanded

Moved to a dedicated coaching centre in Dharampeth with 4 classrooms. Added Science and Commerce streams. Student strength grew to 150+.

2015 — First 100% Board Result

Achieved 100% pass rate in SSC board for the first time. Several students scored above 90%. The institute earned its reputation for consistent results.

2018 — 500 Students Milestone

Crossed 500 active students. Added JEE/NEET Foundation courses and expanded the teaching team. Introduced WhatsApp parent update system.

2022 — Second Centre

Opened second coaching centre to meet growing demand. Total student strength crossed 1000. Introduced Board Exam Crash Courses for Class 10 & 12.

2024 — 1200+ Students

Today, Vijay Tuition Classes serves 1200+ students across 9 different programmes with a 98% consistent pass rate and 50+ board toppers produced.
